Call to Fame

After recognition from a record company, Taylor came out with her first hit, "Tim McGraw". It quickly gained popularity amongst young country music fans and eventually made it into the Billboard Top Charts. After seeing so much success with her first song, Taylor came out with her debut album titled "Taylor Swift". The record features songs called "Tim McGraw", "Picture to Burn", "Teardrops on My Guitar", "A Place in This World", "Cold As You", "The Outside", "Tied Together with a Smile", "Stay Beautiful", "Should've Said No", "Mary's Song", "Our Song", "I'm Only Me When I'm With You", "Invisible", and "A Perfectly Good Heart". Her album was very well recieved and she sold millions of records at only seventeen years old.
