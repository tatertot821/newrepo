Current Life

In the last four years, Taylor released four new albums and two re-recorded albums. In 2019, she released "Lover", in 2020, she released the sister albums, "folklore" and "evermore", in 2021, she released her two re-records, "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" and "Red (Taylor's Version)", and in 2022, she released "Midnights". Taylor has reached a status no other artists have aspired to. Now the winner of eleven Grammy's and more than five hundred other awards, she is one of the most famed singers of all time. Taylor is as busy as ever as she had just began her new tour, "Taylor Swift's The Eras Tour". In her shows, she takes fans through all of her ten eras, "Taylor Swift", "Fearless", "Speak Now", "Red", "1989", "reputation", "Lover", "folklore", "evermore", and "Midnights".
